## Exact renderer root inventory

the two public trait impls, then compares them byte-for-byte with both
`tools/size-report-renderer-roots.txt` and the compiled consumer harness. Any
addition, removal, or unrepresented method fails before linking.

That is 31 audited entry points: 16 inherent, seven `Factory`, and eight
`Renderer`. The hidden consumer root is excluded from the shipped C header and
normal SDK features; the final link verifies that exactly one compiled consumer
root survives.
